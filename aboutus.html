About Us
Flakes is the current leading film production company in the market. With the best and most trend setting movies such as Puss In Boots and Avatar, Flakes has been creating trending and popular flicks since the early 200s. Founded by an ex-Disney animator, Flakes started off as a platform for people to showcase and make their own animations and films. Now Flakes has become more than just a website to share animations, creating more than 50 in house movies and animations that are showing on theatres and on the website today.
